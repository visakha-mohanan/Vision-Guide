About Us

VisionGuide is an all-in-one platform dedicated to protecting and enhancing eye health. It provides advanced image-based analysis to detect potential eye conditions, generates detailed PDF reports, and connects users with nearby eye care facilities for timely consultation. Beyond diagnostics, VisionGuide promotes healthy visual habits by monitoring screen time and sending reminders based on the 20-20-20 rule, empowering users to maintain long-term eye wellness—all in one seamless platform.
